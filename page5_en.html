Testimonies


  • Maëlle (Momoyama University in Osaka)

Maëlle, LEA english-japanese, L3, Osaka

"I left for a semester at Momoyama University in Osaka, Japan. The care of students is very well managed, we are greeted right out of the plane, and Resident Assistant speaking English can assist you in the daily residence hall at your disposal both to show you how to pay your bills and to organize outings cultural. You can choose your subjects whose courses are taught in English and the Japanese courses (mandatory) split between four levels can really progress. The Buddy system assigns you a Japanese student who will be happy to talk to you and allow you to make Japanese friends. The highly developed community life is also open to foreign students and is a good way to meet Japanese people and get acquainted with their way of life. I will recommend this exchange warmly to anyone who really wants to progress in Japanese and live an experience exceptional."

- by Maëlle

Olivier, LEA english-japanese, L3, Osaka

"The Osaka Prefectural University is a place for higher education and personal development. First, it offers an incredible range of teaching (economics, engineering, studies of society ...), but also countless clubs where all students, whatever their department, meet (music, swimming, baseball, diving ...). In addition, students have at their disposal, computer rooms located on campus, where they have the opportunity to work and conduct their research, as well as in the library.Finally, faculty staff, including faculty, are available to their students, and accompany them on the path that leads them to graduation. "

- by Olivier
