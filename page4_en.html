Graduate studies are made to travel at reduced cost

Graduate studies allow you to travel, discover new regions and cultures of the world while continuing your education. In addition, the destinations offered to French students are very advantageous but can sometimes have a cost well above your budget. Fortunately, we have solutions for you.

Scholarships in general

It should be noted that fellows can accumulate scholarships during a traineeship or during a semester abroad.

The best-known scholarship to study abroad is certainly Erasmus. But, if you are a fellowship on social criteria, a international mobility aid in addition to your scholarship can also be a solution.

Other help devices, less known, can also be interesting. Also think about possible financial help from your commune, department or region.

Erasmus

Erasmus is the European student exchange program that allows you to study in Europe. The network of contacts ensures a good reception and a daily accompaniment in your studies abroad.

An Erasmus Scholarship can be awarded to you if you are doing part of your studies in a European foreign country, with an institution of higher education or in the frame of an inter-establishment exchange.

All French universities and the majority of other higher education institutions participate in the Erasmus program. Each institution defines its procedures and schedule.

Types of Erasmus Scholarships:

  • Erasmus studies
    • Reserved for students of the Erasmus program
    • The duration of the allowance is from 3 to 12 months
    • The amount of the Erasmus grant is 250 € to 300 € / month
  • Erasmus traineeship
    • Reserved for students doing an internship in Europe
    • The duration of the allowance is 2 to 12 months
    • The amount of the allowance is 400 € to 450 € / month

Scholarships on social criteria

Students from France or from another European Union country wishing to study in a Council of Europe member state may receive a scholarship on social criteria under the same conditions as in France.

These students must be enrolled in an institution of higher education officially recognized by that country and follow one academic year or two semesters of graduate studies leading to a national diploma.

International mobility aid

The international mobility aid allows you to follow a higher education abroad as part of an exchange program or to do an international internship.

The international mobility aid (ie 400 € per month) can be attributed to you if you fill the following conditions :

  • Your stay abroad lasts between 2 and 9 consecutive months.

    During all of your higher education, you can not accumulate more than 9 months of international mobility assistance.

  • You are a scholarship holder of higher education on social criteria or beneficiary of an annual allowance (device of specific aids)

  • You are preparing a national diploma within the competence of the Ministry of Higher Education.

  • Your training or internship abroad is part of your course of study.

If you fulfill these conditions, you simply have to send to the international relations department from your school a request for mobility assistance , with a project of study stay or international internships.

Insufficient scholarships

If all the scholarships and / or helpers are not enough or if you do not fulfill the conditions you still have a chance to finance your semester with the student jobs. They are of all types and depend on the country where you are going.

The most popular student jobs are in the restoration / fast food, you can also have job offers in the establishment where you go. (language courses, secretarial ...)

You also have the opportunity to do a 0% student loan with your bank but the conditions are many and repayment is often complicated.
